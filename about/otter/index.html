Otter

Hi there!

I am a yellow Labradore retriever, and for the past 4 years I have been providing guiding services to my client Hussain Kadhem. I graduated Summa cum laude in September 2016 from a two-year vocational training program at the Guide Dogs For the Blind school in San Rafael, California.

On My Work

I work with my client on a daily basis to provide travel safety and navigation services. These involve taking point on negotiation of tough pedestrian and urban issues, such as phones claiming monopolies on the pedestrian attention market, and automobiles in teritorial disputes with sidewalks.

On Humans
